Lithium Disilicate Over Zirconia Bridge

  • Maxillary arch esthetic case with bridges
  • HT e.max press material
  • Hybrid Lithium Disilicate over Zirconia bridges

 

 

This 1 hour and 15 minute video integrates two bridges into an esthetic reconstructive case. Smart Implant Clinical Technique

  • Virtually planned guided surgery
  • Immediate loading with a metal reinforced bridge

This video presented by Dr. John Roberts features immediate placement and immediate loading of implants replacing a denture. This technique involves treatment planning using cat scan data, the creation of a model from the digital data, then the fabrication of a bridge, bridge carrier and surgical guide that allow an all in one appointment approach to a denture to fixed bridge conversion for a patient. This technique allows for the try in of the bridge prior to placement of the implants, thus insuring an outcome that is aesthetically and functionally successful. This process has many unique procedures and a very high predictability level.
