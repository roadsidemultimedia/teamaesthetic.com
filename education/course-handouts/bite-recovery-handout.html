Bite Recovery Handout

  1. New poly-vinyl impressions of provisional restorations are taken
  2. New face bow of provisional may be taken or original face bow mounting of prepared teeth may be used,
  3. New posterior bite registrotions are token of the unprepared molars with provisionals in place.
  4. Anterior provisional to provisional registrations are taken with the posterior bites in place. Care needs to be token to ensure full closure into the posterior bites in oil following steps.
  5. Two to four lower anterior provisionals are removed.
